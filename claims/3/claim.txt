================================================================
Claim 3 — Full virtualization sharply reduces raw-PII egress
          while largely preserving utility (paper §3)
================================================================

Statement
----------------------------------------------------------------
With the full piiv virtualization pipeline active (regex +
per-language OPF v2 fine-tune, vault, broker, leak guard), an LLM
tool-calling agent preserves most baseline tool-call utility while
reducing cumulative raw-PII transmissions to the model by more than
99.5%. The remaining bounded residual is the code-switched typed-
identifier failure mode documented in the paper.

Headline numbers from the paper
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Baseline raw-PII transmissions:        17,631
  Virtualization raw-PII transmissions:      65
  Reduction:                              99.63%

  Per-model baseline raw-PII:          2,066-2,311
  Per-model virtualization raw-PII:         4-12

  Average TSR across the eight models:
    baseline:        ~92.3%
    virtualization:  ~90.5%
    delta:           ~-1.8pp

  Seven of eight models are within 5.5 percentage points of baseline
  under virtualization. The outlier is claude-haiku-4.5 (-20.3pp),
  driven mostly by its Russian-language slice.

Acceptance bounds
----------------------------------------------------------------
This claim has two reproduction modes:

  SMOKE (default, ~5 min, ~$1):
    1 LLM (claude-haiku-4.5) x English x 10 queries.
    Acceptance: the run completes and produces a results JSON; the
    virtualization TSR is in [0.7, 1.0]. Small-N raw-PII counts should
    be inspected but are not used as the full-paper bound.

  FULL (--full flag, ~16-20h, ~$20-50):
    8 LLMs x 3 languages x 3 pipelines x 309 queries.
    Acceptance:
      - aggregated mean Δ TSR (virtualization - baseline) is in
        [-5pp, +5pp] across the 8 models;
      - virtualization cumulative raw-PII transmissions are < 100;
      - raw-PII reduction versus baseline is > 99.5%;
      - leak-guard trigger rate remains 0.0% in every cell.

    Note: the reference run's per-cell JSON / MD outputs already ship
    under benchmarks/pii_evaluation/results/. To force a fresh
    reproduction rather than re-aggregate cached cells, delete
    results_*__opf-{en,de,ru}.{json,md} before running.

What `run.sh` does
----------------------------------------------------------------
By default, runs a single English smoke cell:
  python -m benchmarks.pii_evaluation.run_experiment
    --language en --limit 10
    --second-pass opf --opf-model en
    --llm-model anthropic/claude-haiku-4.5

With `--full`, dispatches the full 24-cell matrix (8 LLMs × 3
languages) and runs the Section 3 aggregators and forensics report.

Requires PII_EVAL_API_KEY in .env, set to an OpenRouter key
(format: sk-or-v1-...). Without the key, the script exits with
a clear error and instructions.
